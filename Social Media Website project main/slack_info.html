Slack

Introduction

Slack is a type of instant messaging platform, like WhatsApp, Snapchat and Messenger, developed by Slack Technologies. It is meant to help people and their teams work together more seamlessly. It was designed this way in order to help support the way people naturally work together, so that they can collaborate with other people online as efficiently as you do face-to-face. The main part of Slack is called the workspace, which is made of different channels, each belonging to a different team member where they communicate and talk to each other as well as send messages and share files. You’re even able open private chats between you and your team and start a voice messages or video call. The messages are almost instant so not only can you talk to one another but have full blown conversations.

How to Register

There are three main ways of joining a slack workspace. However, if you simply want to create a Slack workspace, go to https://slack.com/create#email and enter your email address and you will get a 6-digit confirmation code and enter your code. Then name your workspace to create a new channel for your workspace and add your staffs’ email addresses. Click “See Your Channel” in Slack to visit the workspace then select “Finish Signing Up” at the top of the screen and enter your name and password and click “Finish” to save.

  1. Accept an email invitation from someone in the workplace.
  2. Join your company's workspace automatically using your email address.
  3. You can find workspaces you are eligible to join by looking up your email address.

Option one has two steps to complete. First, you must click “Join Now” on the invitation and enter your full name and a password, then click” Create Account”.

Option two where you can join automatically has also a few more steps to do. First go onto https://slack.com/get-started#/, then click “Find your workspace” and enter the approved email address. You will then get an email from slack to confirm your email address and lastly, click “join” to create your account and sign in.

The third and final option is to find any workspace you are eligible to join, and you just have to go to https://slack.com/get-started#/ and then click “Find your Slack workspaces”.

How to use the App/Tool

Click the search bar in the upper left corner to open the search window and type anything that you are looking for and press enter. The channels are used for interacting with people in your workspace. You can also create your own channel or join an existing one and use the channels to talk about certain topics to work on certain projects. The members can also join any public channels if they want to, but they must receive an invitation to use private channels. Directs messages are used to send a message privately to another member of the workspace. You can also create group direct messages and add any member of your workspace into it.

Slack Application increases the power of your workspace. By using the applications in Slack, it offers you a new functions and services within one platform which makes it easier for the members to manage their workload. You can also use the provided API’s to read, write and update data on Slack. You can make a call and videoconferences to have discussions, share information and work on projects more efficiently with other members. Videoconferencing offers you the option to work from home with people anywhere in the world. You can also share your screen with other members during the videoconference.

For Education

There are many ways for a teacher /lecturer utilize social media in order to assist them teach with teaching their class. For instance, social media gives lecturers a chance to post homework online when they are unable to do so in class. It also allows the college to communicate to students and their families about upcoming events. Social is also very helpful in helping students engage further with the lecture materials.

A student can easily send a lecturer an email about an issue, instead of having to meet them face to face. It is more convenient than having to arrange a meeting a later time and having to wait days for an appointment since the lecturer can easily send a response within minutes of receiving an email. Lecturers can also upload lecture videos in Slack for people who were unable to attend lectures. Monitoring a student progress is also something social media is very useful for a prime example of this is Moodle.

As a mentioned, Slack can also be used for things other than study groups as it gives the lecturer a chance to interact with students outside of school, giving the students access to the most up to date information about work and events, for example the date when the grades are going to be released.

One way that Slack can be used for teaching is through the creation of study groups when making groups assignments. This way, groups can communicate and help each other in the comfort of their own homes. They can easily send files and emails to each other, exchange ideas and get feedback from group members. Since communication in Slack is instant, you don’t have to worry about any lags.

Cautions and Safety

Introduction

When approaching to use any social media app there can be some issues, challenges and safety problems that can occur. What is most important is that you're using the app responsibly and safely. Slack's Mission statement is ‘Making work simpler, more pleasant, and more productive.’ which they try to promote very much.

Advice on safety precautions

When using any application, you must be careful on what you should share such as do not share too much information in any social media tool. Slack can be used as an app for business and revealing too much about yourself or your life can seem somewhat unprofessional. In ties with this point, be careful on how you text or say as everyone can view this information. To use the app Slack, you must be older than 16 years old.

Security

Tips that can help when dealing with privacy from this tool:

  • Whenever using any social media app never share any personal details like passwords, financial information.
  • Whenever using the app to communicate, remember that people can see directly who is talking so you must be careful what you say or put in that chats.

Challenges of using Slack

When using Slack there is not many challenges that have been documented or that we can find and there really is only a few:

  • It can become quite messy and you can start to lose track of things because of multiple different conversations.
  • The layout of the channel conversations can cause difficulty as one conversation after another can be hard in sustaining a full conversation.

How it can be misused:

Slack provides services for all users called ‘Channels’. Channels allows you to communicate to any person or a group through a hashtag set up by an admin. When you join the hashtag, you will become a part of the group channel. For example, #businessclub. In regard to these ‘channels’, they can sometimes become more of an immature group chat rather than a professional outlet where groups of people can share their files, emails or notes.

Trolling/Bullying

Linking in with the use of ‘channels’, bullying can happen as when joining groups with people who have the same interests, but different outlooks can cause conflicts. There is not a lot of bullying documented on the app which is a positive thing.

As slack was created for people to work together to communicate, unfortunately it doesn’t allow you to block, mute or report people. This reason being the app wants you to work together to solve the issue as if you were on a team.
